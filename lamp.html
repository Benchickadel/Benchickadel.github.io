Lamp attachment

The lamp we bought has a screw connection to secure the cord to the socket housing so I used this to hold the lamp connection in place.  The piece I created has a treaded screw (thank you Victor for posting the tutorial).  The threaded screw (and accompanying nut not shown here) holds the clamp in place. The plug and switch *just* fit through the opening so the entire housing can be removed.

Material clamp

This part holds whatever is going to hang down around the light. I designed a few things to be cut in paper and polyester film (more on that in another page).  I used the loft command to create the corrugated surface and the flow command to wrap around a circular curve.  I used this surface as a cutting surface on a cylinder.  I created the pegs and used a simple grasshopper sequence to place them around the base of the clamp.  Baked and union-ed them to the base and then used boolean difference on another cylinder to create the inverse top. The clamp then fits onto the lamp connection and a nut holds it all in place.  I am adjusting the lamp attachment to hold 2 clamps so I can add more hanging things.
