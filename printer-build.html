Build

Unfortunately I did not record or time the actual unboxing and build time of my Ender Pro 3. I would say it was roughly 2 hours to get it physically constructed and then lots of youtube videos to get it level and extruding.

Troubleshooting

The biggest issue I had was my build plate was super wobbly.  It was not apparent from the printed directions included with the printer on how to fix this.  After some Internet digging I found there are 2 eccentric nuts under the build plate that help 'snug' the bearings along the rail.  A 1/4 turn on them both and the plate was super ridged. Here's a video that I found for this https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P5HJgIrgGXc

Extras

I added a few things to the printer to help manage the filament and wires and they seem to help.  The first was a filament guide system.  You can find these parts on Thingiverse.com here and here. I chose these because they just pushed or slotted into place and did not need screws. I also had a skate bearing laying around so I used that.  I also used a cable clip and zip tie on the PTFE tube holders.
